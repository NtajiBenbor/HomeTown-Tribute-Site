Top 3 activities to do in Calabar

Attend the Calabar carnival

Attend Africas largest street party! it is held every year in december. the party is attended by millions of people from all around the world.

Visit the old Residency National National Museum

Vist the old Residency museum and explore the rich history and culture of the Calabar people

Visit the Beautiful Marina Resort

This idylic location situated along the banks of the Calabar river, plays host to hundreds of visitors daily. with stunning views of the calabar marina and beautiful greenery at every turn.
